BUY AN ELECTRIC CAR IN UAE

ORDER ANY ELECTRIC CAR STRAIGHT FROM THE PRODUCER AND GET HOME DELIVERY WITH EASY TRACING ALL THE STEPS ONLINE.

You can buy e car online directly from the manufacturer in China. You only need to follow a few simple steps:

  • * Select a car and check its availability for an order.
  • * If you have already decided the model of the electric car, then just contact us in any convenient way. Our team will check the availability of this vehicle for ordering, as well as the duration of the waiting list.
  • * You may always ask our team for advice to chose the right car.
  • * Once the car is ready to be picked up from a dealer in China and be shipped to UAE we will request an invoice on your name. After paying the bill, you become the full owner of the car.
  • * We also request an invoice from a logistics partner for the transportation of a vehicle from China to the UAE. After paying the bill, the car is sent to your city.
  • * When your vehicle arrives to the UAE our customes broker issues an invoice for the clearance service.
By ordering a vehicle from us, you get an excellent Chinese electric car at appropriate price and save sufficient amount of money.

Here is the list of cities in the United Arab Emirates where you can ask us to deliver the car to.

Dubai, Abu Dhabi, Ras Al Khaimah, Sharjah, Fujairah, Umm al-Quwain, Ajman.

Why choose us?

  • Ordering a car by direct delivery is cheaper for the customer than buying a similar vehicle locally.
  • Step-by-step direct payments minimize risks.
  • A transparent work scheme and support at all stages guarantee safety.
  • We only charge a commission for an escort service. You buy a vehicle from a manufacturer in China and pay the bill directly.
  • E-car delivery is possible to any city on the territory of the United Arab Emirates.
